Chart and table of the China fertility rate from 1950 to 2021. United Nations projections are also included through the year 2100.
  • The current fertility rate for China in 2021 is 1.699 births per woman, a 0.18% increase from 2020.
  • The fertility rate for China in 2020 was 1.696 births per woman, a 0.18% increase from 2019.
  • The fertility rate for China in 2019 was 1.693 births per woman, a 0.18% increase from 2018.
  • The fertility rate for China in 2018 was 1.690 births per woman, a 0.6% increase from 2017.
